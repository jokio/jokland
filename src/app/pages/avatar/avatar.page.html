NFTs

Each Avatar Item (T-Shirt, Glasses, Face Mask, etc.) will represent NFT and you will be able to build your avatar by composing different Avatar Items.

You will have common Avatar Items available for free and you can get additional ones from Packs. Check out Packs page for more info.

Creators

Everyone can be a creator and publish Avatar Packs permissionless way. We will provide all necessary tooling.

Creators will define the price for their collections of Avatar Items. And they will get all the income.

Item Rarity

Each Avatar Item will have its Rarity Level. The Creator will define the item's rarety level based on the supply.

Rarity Levels:
  • Common - Unlimited and available for everyone
  • Uncommon - Limited supply, max: 5000
  • Rare - Limited supply, max: 1000
  • Epic - Limited supply, max: 500
  • Legendary - Limited supply, max: 10 (for legendary users)
  • Artifact - No supply. Only one copy of the item will exist.

Partner Apps

All web3 apps can use the Avatar. Use our js SDK and you will have the avatar of any user (ethereum address).
